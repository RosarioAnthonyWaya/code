Made to Grow

Building the Next Big Business in Badagry

Hosted in proud partnership with The Covenant Nation Badagry, this summit is a growth experience built for the doers — the founders, the creators, and the business minds ready to step into real structure, scale, and success.

Register here


Many people in Badagry are doing their best with what they have. But truthfully, “doing your best” isn’t always enough. You’ve put in the work — opened your shop, served your customers, taken the risks. Yet somehow, the business is still not growing the way it should. That’s why we’re hosting this summit.

Why This Summit Matters

Because too many hardworking people are stuck.

This event was designed for people who are tired of trial and error..

For those who started small but want to grow bigger — and smarter

For those who are doing business, but don’t yet have the right systems, structure, or support to truly scale.

What You’ll Learn

This is not a typical seminar. You won’t be left guessing what to do. You’ll leave with simple tools and clarity on:

  • How to manage and grow your business

  • Where your money is going — and how to increase profit

  • How to attract the right customers and keep them

  • How to prepare your business for bigger opportunities

You’ll also connect with other serious business owners — people who know what it means to keep pushing, even when it’s hard.

Who is this event for?

We designed this for the real business owners:

  • The tailor who wants to open a second location

  • The agropreneur struggling to scale production

  • The digital freelancer unsure how to charge right

  • The school owner stuck at 50 students

  • The side hustler finally ready to go full-time

  • the shop owner who wants to expand their product line

If you’ve ever thought, “I know my business can do more” — this is where you start.

Who is Hosting?

We're proud to partner with The Covenant Nation Badagry, a church community deeply committed to empowering its people spiritually, economically, and intellectually. Led by a vision for national impact, TCN Badagry has become a hub for people who want to make a real difference in their lives and in their community. This summit is one of many efforts to merge faith with structure, and passion with productivity.

Meet the Host Pastor: Pastor Folorunsho Samuel Ibitoye

Host Pastor, The Covenant Nation Badagry A dynamic teacher, community leader, and business mentor, Pastor Folorunsho Samuel Ibitoye is passionate about raising purpose-driven entrepreneurs. Under his leadership, TCN Badagry has grown into a center of excellence — equipping members not only in spiritual growth, but also in leadership, business, and personal development. He believes Badagry can birth businesses that compete at the highest level — and this summit is part of that belief in action.



Meet the Trainer: Rosario Anthony

Anthony is a business growth strategist, founder of Boltzmenn, and builder of practical growth systems for African entrepreneurs. He’s trained hundreds of entrepreneurs across Nigeria on how to move from daily hustle to structured scale — and he’s bringing his proven frameworks, zero-fluff teaching style, and real-life case studies to this summit. Known for his practical wisdom, Anthony simplifies finance, operations, customer strategy, and funding readiness so that even small businesses can start acting like big businesses — and get the results to match.

Why Boltzmenn?

We don’t believe in giving people hype with no direction. Boltzmenn exists to support African business owners who are trying to build something real — with strategy, not guesswork..


This is our way of saying to Badagry business owners: We see your work. Let’s help you build better. .
