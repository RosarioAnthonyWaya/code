Our story

Why We Started Boltzmenn?

We started Boltzmenn 2.0 because we saw how hard it is to grow a business in Africa. Since 2020, we have embarked on a missiom to make africna businesses become global players.

Join our team


We believe Africans and african businesses can be global industry leaders—if they have the right support and guidance.

We started Boltzmenn 2.0 because we saw the struggle. Great ideas get stuck—not because they lack potential, but because the system is tough. Bad roads, power outages, lack of funding, and little access to real mentorship. We wanted to change that. So we didn’t just build Boltzmenn as one company; we built an ecosystem of growth:


  • Boltzmenn:Our main company, where we give practical advice and support to businesses.

  • Outhbox: Our tech arm, bringing digital solutions and innovation to African businesses, making them ready for the global stage.

  • Sturgeon (Boltzmenn Capital): Access to funding that understands African realities. We don’t just provide capital; we build paths to financial growth.

  • Redenhaus: Our creative and brand studio, shaping African brands to not just compete, but stand out—locally and globally.

  • Startup Kopa: Training fresh graduates to understand the future of work, building the next generation of African business leaders.

We built this ecosystem because we believe African businesses can last for generations. We want to see them grow from small shops to global players, from surviving to thriving. Our mission is simple: build African giants.

“We built boltzmenn to support the wildest African dreams”
